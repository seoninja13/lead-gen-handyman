# Content Guidelines Documentation

## Overview
Guidelines for creating and maintaining content across the handyman website, ensuring SEO optimization and user engagement.

## Content Structure

### 1. Homepage Content (3000+ words)

#### Hero Section
- Clear value proposition
- Location-specific headline
- Call-to-action button
- Trust indicators (reviews, certifications)

#### Service Overview
- Brief description of each main service
- Benefits and unique selling points
- Service area coverage
- Emergency service availability

#### Video Content
- 4-5 embedded YouTube videos
- Topics:
  - Service demonstrations
  - Customer testimonials
  - How-to guides
  - Company overview
- Video requirements:
  - HD quality (minimum 720p)
  - Professional narration
  - Clear branding
  - Closed captions

#### FAQ Section
- 10-15 common questions
- Schema markup implementation
- Categorized by service type
- Clear, concise answers

### 2. Service Category Pages (1500-2000 words)

#### Content Sections
1. Service Introduction
   - Overview
   - Benefits
   - Target audience
   - Service area

2. Service Details
   - Process explanation
   - Equipment/materials used
   - Time requirements
   - Safety considerations

3. Pricing Information
   - Price ranges
   - Factors affecting cost
   - Payment options
   - Guarantees/warranties

4. Service-Specific FAQs
   - 5-7 targeted questions
   - Detailed answers
   - Related service links
   - Expert tips

5. Customer Reviews
   - Recent testimonials
   - Before/after photos
   - Video testimonials
   - Rating summaries

### 3. City-Service Pages

#### Local Content Elements
1. City-Specific Introduction
   - Local service availability
   - Coverage areas
   - Local team information
   - Emergency response times

2. Service Area Details
   - Interactive map
   - Neighborhood coverage
   - Travel time estimates
   - Service restrictions

3. Local Pricing
   - Area-specific rates
   - Local factors
   - Seasonal variations
   - Special offers

4. Local Testimonials
   - Neighborhood-specific reviews
   - Local project examples
   - Community involvement
   - Local certifications
